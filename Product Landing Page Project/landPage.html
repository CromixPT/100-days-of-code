ENDLESS POSSIBILITIES AT YOUR FINGERTIPS

Thermomix ® from Vorwerk is a unique all-in-one kitchen appliance that will change the way you cook by helping to unlock your full culinary potential while saving you time and effort. Its compact design takes up only slightly more surface area on the worktop than an A4-sized sheet of paper, but packs enormous power, versatility and innovation, taking on numerous jobs in the kitchen.

The new Thermomix brings the kitchen into the digital age with a beautiful colour touchscreen display. You can follow onscreen recipe instructions by using the smart Recipe Chip, which is enhanced by the Guided Cooking function. This innovation for guaranteed success takes you step by step through recipes onscreen. The recipes have been tailor made to work perfectly with the appliance – all you need to do is add the ingredients, and your Thermomix ® will set the temperature and timings for you. It’s a simple, fast alternative that complements the ability to operate any function entirely manually and lets you cook your way.
